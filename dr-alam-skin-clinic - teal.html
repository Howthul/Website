Our Equipment

The machines we use
and what they do.

All 9 machines are physically present at the clinic in Erode. The doctor decides which one to use for your skin — not a general staff member.

Every machine listed here is at the clinic. Nothing is outsourced. Treatment settings are decided by the doctor, not by a pre-set programme or a technician.

Scar Removal
Quanta Youlaser MT
CO₂ Laser · Acne Scars · Skin Texture

A high-quality Italian laser used mainly for deep acne scars and uneven skin texture. It removes a very thin layer of damaged skin, allowing fresh, smoother skin to grow in its place.

Pigmentation
Quanta Q-Plus A Evo
Q-switch Laser · Dark Spots · Tattoo Removal

A laser that targets dark pigment in the skin without damaging the surrounding area. Used for dark spots, uneven patches, melasma, and tattoo removal.

Light + Pigment
Quanta Light B Evo
IPL Light Treatment · Skin Brightening

Uses broad-spectrum light to treat scattered pigmentation, skin redness, and sun damage across a wider area. Also useful for skin brightening and refreshing dull skin.

Scars + Pores
Vacuum Microneedling RF
MNRF · Rolling Scars · Skin Tightening

Tiny needles deliver radio-frequency energy under the skin to stimulate collagen production. Used for rolling scars, large pores, and loose skin. More comfortable than older versions of this treatment.

Vitiligo · Psoriasis
Kernel Excimer 308nm
Targeted Light Therapy

A specialised light therapy machine that targets only the affected patches of skin — used for vitiligo (white patches) and psoriasis. Healthy surrounding skin is not exposed to the light.

Skin Growths
Conmed Hyfrecator 2000
Electrosurgery · Skin Tag · Wart Removal

A precision electrosurgical tool used to remove small skin growths — skin tags, warts, moles, and tiny cysts — with minimal scarring when used by a trained doctor.

Hair Removal
Diode Laser
Permanent Hair Reduction · All Skin Tones

A laser that targets hair follicles to reduce hair growth permanently over multiple sessions. Works well on Indian skin tones. Settings are adjusted for your skin colour and hair type.

Acne + Healing
LED Light Therapy
LED PDT · Acne · Skin Recovery

Different colours of LED light have different effects — blue light kills acne-causing bacteria, red light helps skin heal faster, and near-infrared light reduces inflammation. Often used after other treatments to speed up recovery.

Skin Tightening
HIFU
Ultrasound Lifting · Non-surgical Firming

Uses focused ultrasound energy to tighten the deep layers of skin without surgery. Results appear gradually over 2–3 months. The skin looks firmer and lifted without looking "done."
